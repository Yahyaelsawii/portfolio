01

The Problem

Students were relying on the existing platform (“Pulse”), but recurring sign-in errors, delayed notifications, and missing features made it unreliable and frustrating for daily academic use.

The goal was a unified “one place” student experience: quick access to myCourses and the Student Information System (SIS), plus timely alerts and a cleaner navigation structure.

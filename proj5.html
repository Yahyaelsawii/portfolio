01

The Problem

People often track their mood inconsistently and struggle to understand what causes stress over time. The app needed a way to make patterns obvious without overwhelming users.

The biggest UX challenge: turning daily check-ins into meaningful insights using simple visuals and clear time filters.

02

Research & Goals

We focused on what users actually want from mental health tracking: clarity, not complexity.

visibility

Make patterns visible

Users should instantly notice “good weeks vs bad weeks” without digging.

tune

Filters that feel natural

Day / Week / Month toggles should reduce noise, not add friction.

notifications_active

Support, not overwhelm

Stress alerts should be gentle and actionable, suggesting coping steps rather than “warning” users.
